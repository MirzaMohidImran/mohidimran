Shopify and Shopify Plus Development

Overview

I provide complete Shopify and Shopify Plus development services designed for brands that want performance, scalability, and long term growth. From startups launching their first online store to high volume businesses migrating to Shopify Plus, I build secure, fast, and conversion focused e commerce solutions tailored to your business goals. Every Shopify store is developed with clean code, SEO best practices, mobile first design, and seamless integrations to ensure smooth customer experiences and high conversion rates.

Shopify Store Setup and Configuration

I handle full Shopify store setup from scratch including store configuration, theme setup, product structure, collections, taxes, shipping, and payment gateways. Whether you are starting a new business or rebranding an existing one, I ensure your Shopify store is structured correctly for growth and search engine visibility.

Custom Shopify Theme Development

Your brand deserves a unique storefront. I develop fully custom Shopify themes using Liquid, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This includes custom layouts, sections, and reusable components that match your brand identity while maintaining performance and scalability. All themes are lightweight, responsive, and optimized for speed and SEO.

Shopify Plus Development for High Growth Brands

For businesses scaling rapidly or handling high traffic and large order volumes, I offer Shopify Plus development services. This includes advanced theme customization, Shopify Scripts, checkout customization, automation workflows, and enterprise level integrations. Shopify Plus solutions are built to support global selling, multi store management, and high performance infrastructure without compromising user experience.

Shopify Migration

I provide secure and seamless migration from platforms such as WooCommerce, Magento, Wix, BigCommerce, and custom systems to Shopify or Shopify Plus. This includes product data, customer records, orders, URLs, and SEO preservation to ensure zero data loss and minimal downtime.

Shopify App Integration and Custom Functionality

I integrate essential Shopify apps and third party tools to extend store functionality including payment gateways, shipping providers, CRM systems, inventory tools, analytics platforms, and marketing automation. For advanced needs, I also develop custom features and workflows tailored to your business processes.

Shopify Performance Optimization

Store speed directly impacts conversions and search rankings. I optimize Shopify stores for fast loading times through performance improvements, asset optimization, code cleanup, and mobile responsiveness. This results in improved user experience, reduced bounce rates, and higher conversion rates.

Shopify SEO Optimization

All Shopify stores are built with SEO friendly architecture and best practices. This includes optimized URLs, meta titles, descriptions, schema setup, clean code structure, image optimization, internal linking, and technical SEO configuration. My goal is to help your Shopify store rank higher on search engines and attract consistent organic traffic.

Shopify Automation and Workflow Optimization

I implement automation solutions to streamline store operations such as order processing, inventory updates, customer segmentation, and reporting. This reduces manual effort and improves efficiency using smart Shopify workflows and integrations.

Shopify Maintenance and Support

I provide ongoing Shopify support, updates, bug fixes, and feature enhancements. Whether you need regular maintenance or long term collaboration, I ensure your store remains secure, updated, and optimized.

frequently asked questions

  • What Shopify services do you provide?
    I provide Shopify store setup, custom theme development, Shopify Plus development, migrations, app integrations, automation, performance optimization, and SEO services.
  • What is the difference between Shopify and Shopify Plus?
    Shopify Plus is designed for high growth and enterprise level businesses. It offers advanced customization, automation tools, and scalability features not available in standard Shopify plans.
  • How long does it take to complete a project?
    I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and other modern web development frameworks and tools.
  • How long does it take to build a Shopify store?
    A standard Shopify store usually takes one to two weeks. Advanced or Shopify Plus projects may take three to six weeks depending on complexity.
  • Are Shopify stores mobile friendly?
    Yes. All Shopify and Shopify Plus stores are fully responsive and optimized for all devices.
